Pitcher Sangria

A refreshing and fruity sangria recipe for a 32 oz pitcher.

Ingredients:

  • 16 oz brandy
  • 144 oz red wine
  • 96 oz orange juice or apple juice
  • Fresh muddled fruit

Instructions:

  1. In a 32 oz pitcher, add fresh muddled fruit.
  2. Pour 16 oz of brandy into the pitcher.
  3. Add 144 oz of red wine.
  4. Pour 96 oz of orange juice or apple juice over the fruit and wine.
  5. Stir gently to combine.
  6. Enjoy your delicious Pitcher Sangria!
