Mashed Avocado Bowl

Mashed avocado is topped with fresh veggies, pickled onions, feta cheese, crispy chickpeas, and a hard-boiled egg to create this mashed avocado bowl. It makes for a quick and healthy salad or dip served with tortilla chips.

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Total Time: 15 minutes | Servings: 3 | Author: Brittany Mullins

Ingredients

  • Mashed avocado
  • 3 large avocado, ripe
  • 4.5 Tablespoon lemon juice
  • Pinch of kosher salt
  • Toppings
  • 1.5 cup romaine lettuce, finely chopped
  • 0.75 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 0.75 cup cucumber, diced
  • 3 Tablespoon chopped pickled red onion
  • 6 Tablespoons crumbled feta cheese
  • 3-6 Tablespoons roasted and salted sunflower seeds
  • 0.75 cup crispy chickpeas, store-bought
  • 3 hard-boiled egg, sliced
  • Apple Cider Vinaigrette Dressing

Instructions

  1. In a shallow bowl (glass if possible), mash avocado with lemon juice and salt.
  2. Add toppings in layers, in the order listed above. Drizzle with dressing.
  3. Enjoy immediately with a fork or as a dip with chips.
