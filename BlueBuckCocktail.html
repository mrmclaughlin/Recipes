Blue Buck Cocktail Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Whisky (use a smooth, versatile whisky that mixes well)
  • 1/2 oz Freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/2 oz Blueberry syrup (homemade or store-bought)
  • Ginger beer to top up (about 3-4 oz)
  • Fresh blueberries for garnish
  • Lemon wheel or peel for garnish
  • Ice

Homemade Blueberry Syrup (Optional)

Ingredients:
- 1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries
- 1 cup water
- 1 cup sugar

Instructions:
1. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine blueberries, water, and sugar.
2. Bring to a simmer and cook until the blueberries burst and release their juice, about 10-15 minutes.
3. Strain the mixture through a fine mesh sieve, pressing on the solids to extract as much liquid as possible. Allow to cool before use.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Blueberry Syrup (if making homemade):
    Follow the instructions provided above.
  2. Make the Blue Buck:
    - In a shaker filled with ice, combine whisky, freshly squeezed lemon juice, and blueberry syrup.
    - Shake well until the mixture is well chilled, about 15-20 seconds.
    - Strain into an ice-filled highball glass.
  3. Finish and Serve:
    - Top up the glass with ginger beer, gently stirring to combine.
    - Garnish with a few fresh blueberries and a lemon wheel or peel.
    - Enjoy your refreshing and unique Blue Buck cocktail!
