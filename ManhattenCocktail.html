Manhattan Cocktail

A classic cocktail known for its bold and rich flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz bourbon whiskey
  • 1 oz sweet vermouth
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Maraschino cherry for garnish
  • Orange twist for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fill a mixing glass with ice.
  2. Add 2 oz of bourbon whiskey to the mixing glass.
  3. Add 1 oz of sweet vermouth to the mixing glass.
  4. Add 2 dashes of Angostura bitters to the mixing glass.
  5. Stir the ingredients well until chilled.
  6. Strain the mixture into a chilled martini or cocktail glass.
  7. Garnish with a maraschino cherry and an orange twist.
  8. Enjoy your classic Manhattan cocktail!
