Frozen Aperol Paloma

A delightful frozen cocktail to cool you down on a sunny day.

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz grapefruit juice
  • 6 oz tequila
  • 3 oz Aperol
  • 2 oz lime juice
  • 2 oz agave syrup
  • 4 cups ice
  • Grapefruit slices for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine 8 oz of grapefruit juice, 6 oz of tequila, 3 oz of Aperol, 2 oz of lime juice, and 2 oz of agave syrup.
  2. Add 4 cups of ice to the blender.
  3. Blend until the mixture is smooth and slushy.
  4. Pour the Frozen Aperol Paloma into glasses.
  5. Garnish each glass with a slice of grapefruit.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
