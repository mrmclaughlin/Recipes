Mother Lover Cocktail

A delightful cocktail with a blend of flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 oz vodka
  • 1 oz Pamplemousse liqueur
  • 0.5 oz elderflower liqueur
  • 0.5 oz lime juice
  • 0.5 oz cranberry juice
  • 0.25 oz simple syrup

Instructions:

  1. Fill a Collins glass with ice.
  2. Pour 1 oz of vodka into the glass.
  3. Add 1 oz of Pamplemousse liqueur.
  4. Pour 0.5 oz of elderflower liqueur into the glass.
  5. Squeeze 0.5 oz of fresh lime juice into the mix.
  6. Add 0.5 oz of cranberry juice.
  7. Finish with 0.25 oz of simple syrup.
  8. Stir gently to combine all the ingredients.
  9. Enjoy your delicious Mother Lover Cocktail!
