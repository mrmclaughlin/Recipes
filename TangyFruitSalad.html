Tangy Fruit Salad

Ingredients

Dressing:

  • 1 chili
  • 1 tsp tamarind paste
  • 1 tsp Parma sugar
  • 1 lime
  • 100 g peanuts
  • Salt

Salad:

  • 1/2 pineapple
  • 1 apple
  • 1 pear
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 mango

Cooking Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the dressing. Slice the chili in half and finely chop it, removing half of the seeds if you prefer less heat.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the chopped chili, tamarind paste, Parma sugar, and the zest and juice of the lime. Set aside.
  3. In a dry pan, toast the peanuts until they are nicely browned. Crush them using a rolling pin or a pan wrapped in a clean cloth.
  4. For the fruit salad, start by preparing the pineapple. Remove the top and bottom, then shave off the skin. Slice the pineapple into chunks.
  5. Dice the apple and pear, and deseed and dice the cucumber. Slice the mango into nice pieces.
  6. Arrange all the prepared fruits in a bowl. Drizzle the dressing over the fruit salad, ensuring all the fruits are coated.
  7. Enjoy this tangy and spicy fruit salad as a refreshing breakfast, lunch, or healthy snack!
