Baked Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups long grain white rice, UNCOOKED (Note 1)
  • 2 cups chicken or veg stock, low sodium
  • 1 1/2 tbsp light soy sauce (Note 2)
  • 1 tbsp Chinese cooking wine (dry sherry, mirin or sake, Note 3)
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced with crusher (straight into pan)
  • 2 cups frozen diced veg (carrots, peas, corn mix, Note 4 fresh veg)
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper
  • 1 1/4 cups / 180g bacon (uncooked) or ham, chopped (I use store bought chopped bacon, Note 5)

Finishing

  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 cup green onion, finely sliced (2 ‚Äì 3 stems)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200¬∞C / 390¬∞F (180¬∞C fan).
  2. Mix ingredients in baking dish: Place all Baked Fried Rice ingredients except bacon in a baking dish (~22 x 32cm / 9 x 13"). Stir, then spread rice evenly across pan.
  3. Top with bacon: Scatter bacon across surface.
  4. Bake covered 40 min: Cover with foil. Bake 40 minutes.
  5. Bake uncovered 15 min: Remove foil. Bake uncovered 15 minutes ‚Äì this will colour the bacon. Liquid should be absorbed, rice should be cooked (but will seem wet, will absorb and soften while resting).
  6. Rest 10 min: Cover loosely with foil, stand for 10 minutes.
  7. Fluff: Add green onion and sesame oil. Fluff rice with fork, marvel at how perfectly cooked the rice grains are. Serve!
