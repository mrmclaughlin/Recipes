Fiesta Chicken

TOTAL TIME: Prep: 15 min. Bake: 40 min.

YIELD: 8 servings

Chili powder and picante sauce add just the right dash to this hearty main dish. It's a snap to assemble since it uses convenience foods. —Teresa Peterson, Kasson, Minnesota

Ingredients

  • 1 can (10-3/4 ounces) condensed cream of chicken soup, undiluted
  • 1 can (10-3/4 ounces) condensed cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
  • 2 small tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/3 cup picante sauce
  • 1 medium green pepper, chopped
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 to 3 teaspoons chili powder
  • 12 corn tortillas (6 inches), cut into 1-inch strips
  • 3 cups cubed cooked chicken
  • 1 cup shredded Colby cheese

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the soups, tomatoes, picante sauce, green pepper, onion, and chili powder. In a greased 13x9-in. baking dish, layer half of the tortilla strips, chicken, soup mixture, and cheese. Repeat layers.
  2. Cover and bake at 350° until bubbly, 40-50 minutes.
