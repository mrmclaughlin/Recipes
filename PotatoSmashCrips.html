Potato Smash Crisps

Crispy and flavorful potato crisps that are perfect for a snack or a side dish!

Servings: 5

Total Time: 1 hour 30 minutes

Author: Unknown

Ingredients

  • 5 Russet potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Shredded cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Butter (for topping)
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Place a sheet of tin foil on a baking sheet and grease it with olive oil.
  3. Cut the potatoes in half along the thinnest side. Dry the cut side of the potatoes with a paper towel and place them on the tin foil.
  4. Brush the potatoes with olive oil and sprinkle salt on the skin side.
  5. Bake for 1 hour.
  6. Raise the oven temperature to 400°F (200°C).
  7. Remove the potatoes from the oven and smash them flat with a glass.
  8. Cover each potato with shredded cheddar cheese.
  9. Place tabs of butter on the tray around the potatoes.
  10. Bake for an additional 30 minutes at 400°F (200°C).
  11. Remove from the oven and let cool to crisp up.
  12. Sprinkle with chopped parsley and serve.
