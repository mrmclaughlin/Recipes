Pamatini

A delightful cocktail made with Pama, lemon vodka, orange juice, and topped with prosecco, garnished with a lemon twist.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 oz Pama
  • 1.5 oz lemon vodka
  • Splash of orange juice
  • Splash of prosecco
  • Lemon twist for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice.
  2. Add 1.5 oz of Pama and 1.5 oz of lemon vodka to the shaker.
  3. Add a splash of orange juice to the shaker.
  4. Shake vigorously until well chilled.
  5. Strain the cocktail into a martini glass.
  6. Top with a splash of prosecco.
  7. Garnish with a lemon twist.
  8. Enjoy your refreshing Pamatini!
