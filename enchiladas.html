Black Bean and Chicken Enchiladas

These Black Bean and Chicken Enchiladas are a delicious and satisfying meal that's perfect for dinner. With tender chicken, flavorful black beans, and gooey cheese, they're sure to become a family favorite!

Servings: 4

Total Time: 45 minutes

Author: Mr. McLaughlin

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb Chicken
  • 1 cup Chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon Cumin
  • 1 tablespoon Chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 teaspoon Pepper
  • 1 can Black beans
  • 2 cups Shredded cheese
  • Corn tortillas
  • Enchilada sauce (Recipe here)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350째F (175째C).
  2. In a pot, combine the chicken, cumin, chili powder, and chicken broth. Cook on medium heat until it reaches a slow boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and continue cooking until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165째F (75째C) and shreds easily. Shred the chicken.
  3. In another pot, cook the black beans with a little water over medium heat until soft.
  4. Wrap a stack of corn tortillas in damp paper towels or a damp kitchen towel, then wrap in plastic wrap or place in a microwave-safe resealable plastic bag (keep the bag open to vent). Microwave until warm and flexible, about 1 minute.
  5. Spread a ladle of enchilada sauce in a 9x12-inch baking dish.
  6. Divide the shredded chicken among half of the tortillas and the black beans among the other half. Add shredded cheese to each.
  7. Roll up the tortillas and place them in the baking dish. Use a bit of enchilada sauce to hold them together if needed.
  8. Cover the enchiladas with the remaining enchilada sauce and sprinkle with more shredded cheese.
  9. Bake for 30 minutes, or until the edges are crispy and the cheese is bubbly.
  10. Serve with rice and a salad.
