Christmas Punch

A festive punch perfect for celebrating Christmas.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cranberry juice
  • 1 cup pomegranate juice
  • 1 cup vodka
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1/2 cup triple sec

Instructions:

  1. In a large punch bowl, combine 2 cups of cranberry juice.
  2. Add 1 cup of pomegranate juice and 1 cup of vodka to the bowl.
  3. Slice 1 lemon and add the lemon slices to the punch.
  4. Pour in 1/2 cup of triple sec.
  5. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
  6. Chill the Christmas Punch in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving.
  7. When ready to serve, pour the punch into glasses with ice.
  8. Garnish with additional lemon slices if desired.
  9. Enjoy your festive Christmas Punch!
