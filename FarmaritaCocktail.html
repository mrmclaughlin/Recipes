Farmarita

A refreshing cocktail with the flavors of carrot juice, orange juice, and tequila.

Servings: 2

Ingredients:

  • 4 oz carrot juice
  • 3 oz orange juice
  • 3 oz tequila
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • 0.5 oz agave

Instructions:

  1. In a cocktail shaker, combine 4 oz of carrot juice.
  2. Add 3 oz of orange juice to the shaker.
  3. Measure and add 3 oz of tequila.
  4. Pour in 1 oz of lime juice.
  5. Add 0.5 oz of agave syrup to the mixture.
  6. Shake the ingredients well in the shaker with ice.
  7. Strain the mixture into two glasses filled with ice.
  8. Garnish with a slice of carrot or a lime wedge, if desired.
  9. Enjoy your Farmarita!
