Banana Tarte Tatin

Ingredients

  • 3 bananas
  • 1 sheet of puff pastry
  • 150 g butter
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon pink peppercorns
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 1 vanilla bean

Cooking Instructions

  1. Place 1 teaspoon of pink peppercorns and 1 teaspoon of black peppercorns in a mortar and pestle. Add the seeds from 1 vanilla bean. Grind together.
  2. Preheat the oven to 190 degrees Celsius (375 degrees Fahrenheit).
  3. Take an oven-safe pan and cover the bottom with slices of butter. Sprinkle the ground vanilla and peppercorns over the butter. Then, sprinkle 2 tablespoons of sugar over the butter and spices.
  4. Cut the bananas into 5 cm pieces and tightly wedge them into the butter in the pan. Sprinkle some peppercorns over the bananas.
  5. Roll out the puff pastry and cut a round piece slightly larger than the pan. Cover the pan with the puff pastry and tuck the edges under the bananas.
  6. Make several holes in the pastry with a knife.
  7. Place the pan on the stove over medium heat and cook for 5 minutes, until the caramel begins to form.
  8. Transfer the pan to the preheated oven and bake for 20 minutes, or until the pastry is golden brown.
  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly. Carefully invert the tart onto a serving plate.
  10. Serve warm and enjoy!
