Benny Mule Cocktail

A refreshing and fruity twist on the classic Moscow Mule.

Ingredients:

  • 6 oz ginger beer
  • 1.5 oz raspberry vodka
  • 0.5 oz Pama pomegranate liqueur
  • Lime slice for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fill a copper mug or glass with ice.
  2. Pour 1.5 oz of raspberry vodka over the ice.
  3. Add 0.5 oz of Pama pomegranate liqueur to the glass.
  4. Top off with 6 oz of ginger beer.
  5. Stir gently to combine the ingredients.
  6. Garnish with a slice of lime.
  7. Enjoy your refreshing Benny Mule cocktail!
