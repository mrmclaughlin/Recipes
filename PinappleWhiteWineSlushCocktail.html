Pineapple White Wine Slush

A refreshing slushy drink perfect for a warm day.

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  • 2 oz elderflower liqueur
  • 4 cups Chardonnay or Pinot
  • 1/2 cup ice
  • Pineapple slices for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine 1/2 cup of pineapple juice and 2 oz of elderflower liqueur.
  2. Add 4 cups of Chardonnay or Pinot to the blender.
  3. Toss in 1/2 cup of ice.
  4. Blend until the mixture is smooth and slushy.
  5. Pour the Pineapple White Wine Slush into glasses.
  6. Garnish each glass with a slice of pineapple.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
