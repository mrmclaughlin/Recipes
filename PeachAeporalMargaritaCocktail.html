Peach Aperol Margarita

A delightful cocktail with the flavors of peach, Aperol, and tequila.

Servings: 1

Ingredients:

  • 1 peach
  • 0.75 oz lime juice
  • 1.5 oz tequila
  • 0.5 oz triple sec
  • 1 oz Aperol
  • 0.5 oz hot honey

Instructions:

  1. Cut the peach into small pieces and place them in a cocktail shaker.
  2. Add 0.75 oz of lime juice to the shaker.
  3. Measure and add 1.5 oz of tequila.
  4. Pour in 0.5 oz of triple sec.
  5. Add 1 oz of Aperol to the mixture.
  6. Measure 0.5 oz of hot honey and add it to the shaker.
  7. Shake the ingredients vigorously in the shaker without ice.
  8. Strain the mixture into a glass filled with muddled peach.
  9. Garnish with a peach slice or a lime wedge, if desired.
  10. Enjoy your Peach Aperol Margarita!
