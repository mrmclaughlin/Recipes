Thai Green Curry Rice - Gordon Ramsay Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Curry paste:
    • 2 large green chilis
    • 1 garlic clove, peeled and roughly chopped
    • 1 lemongrass stalk, roughly chopped
    • 2 kaffir lime leaves
    • 3 cm piece of fresh ginger, peeled and chopped
    • Small bunch of coriander
    • 1 shallot, peeled and chopped
    • Juice of 1 lime
    • 1 - 2 tbsp vegetable oil
    • Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 300g cooked basmati rice, preferably day old or chilled at least 4 hours
  • Vegetable oil
  • Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Lime wedges to serve

Directions:

  1. First, make the curry paste. Put all the ingredients into a food processor or blender with 1 - 2 tbsp vegetable oil, a pinch of sea salt, and ground pepper, and blitz until smooth.
  2. Place a large frying pan or wok over medium-high heat and add 1 tbsp vegetable oil. When hot, add the curry paste and stir over heat until aromatic (2 - 3 minutes).
  3. Add rice to the pan, stir well, and cook for 4 - 5 minutes until the rice is heated through. If necessary, add another tbsp vegetable oil to prevent rice from sticking to the pan.
  4. Serve with lime wedges.
