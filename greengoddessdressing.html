Green Goddess Dressing

A delightful and flavorful dressing perfect for salads or as a dip!

Servings: N/A

Total Time: 1 hour

Author: Unknown

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 2 teaspoons dried tarragon
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk (or substitute with 1 tablespoon vinegar + milk to make 1/2 cup)
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup minced fresh parsley
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1/4 cup minced chives
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Instructions

  1. Combine lemon juice, water, and dried tarragon in a small bowl and let sit for 15 minutes.
  2. Process the tarragon mixture, buttermilk, yogurt, sour cream, parsley, and minced garlic until smooth.
  3. Stir in the minced chives, salt, and pepper.
  4. Cover and refrigerate for at least one hour (can be refrigerated for up to 4 days; whisk to recombine before using).
