Green Goodness Salad Bowl

A nutritious and delicious salad bowl packed with flavor!

Servings: 3

Total Time: N/A

Author: Unknown

Ingredients

  • 6 cups baby spinach
  • 3/4 cup Green Goddess Dressing
  • 3 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 1/8 cups sautéed broccoli with garlic and thyme
  • 3/4 ripe avocado, thinly sliced
  • 3/8 cup frozen shelled edamame beans, thawed and patted dry
  • 3 tablespoons toasted and chopped pistachios
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Toss spinach with half of the dressing to coat. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Divide the dressed spinach among serving bowls.
  3. Top with shredded chicken, sautéed broccoli, avocado slices, and edamame beans.
  4. Drizzle the remaining dressing over the salad bowls.
  5. Sprinkle with chopped pistachios and serve.
