Pierogi Recipe

Prep Time: ~45 minutes | Cook Time: ~10 minutes| makes 12

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 4 tbsp water (+ extra 2 tbsp)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp cold butter

Directions

  1. Add salt to flour and mix.
  2. Flake in butter to flour.
  3. Make a large well in the bowl.
  4. Add egg, water, and extra water into the well.
  5. Mix with a fork until shaggy, pulling in from the edges quickly.
  6. Knead: Fold towards you, press hard, and turn. Repeat until it passes the finger test.
  7. Finger test: Press your finger into the dough; it should rebound as if untouched. If not, keep kneading.
  8. Let dough rise for 20 minutes.

Rolling Out

  1. Dust the table with flour.
  2. Roll out dough to a 1/16-inch thick sheet.
  3. Cut out rounds using a cutter.
  4. Let excess dough rest while filling the cut pieces.

Filling & Shaping

  1. Hold a round in your left hand.
  2. Place filling in the center, leaving a 1/2-inch gap around the edges.
  3. Moisten the edge with water.
  4. Fold over and seal, working from the center outward.
  5. Optional: Create ridges by pressing with thumbs or braid the edges.

Cooking

  1. Freeze for later or cook immediately.
  2. To cook, drop into boiling water.
  3. Boil until pierogi float, turning a light tan.

Brown Butter Sauce

  1. Melt butter until clarified and milk solids begin to brown.
  2. Scrape off brown bits and let cool.
  3. Add sherry vinegar to deglaze.
  4. Toss cooked pierogi in brown butter and serve.
