Slow Cooker BBQ Chickpea Sloppy Joes

PREP: 10 minutes | COOK: 8 hours | TOTAL: 8 hours 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 15-ounce cans chickpeas, drained
  • 1/2 cup uncooked split red lentils, rinsed
  • 1 small onion, diced (about 1 cup)
  • 3 medium cloves garlic, diced (about 1 tablespoon)
  • 1 15-ounce can tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup dark brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons vegan Worcestershire sauce (can use regular Worcestershire if not vegetarian or vegan)
  • 1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon red crushed pepper flakes
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt, plus more to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, plus more to taste

For the buns

  • 4 hamburger buns*
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil

Toppings (optional)

  • Sliced red onion
  • Dill pickles or sweet pickles

Equipment

  • 3-quart or larger slow cooker

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to the slow cooker.
  2. Cook on low for 8 hours or on high 5-6 hours, until the lentils are cooked through and the mixture thickens.
  3. Open the slow cooker and, using a potato masher, mash about 1/3 of the filling. Give it a good stir, replace the lid, and let it cook for another 30 minutes or so until thickened.
  4. Meanwhile, toast the buns. Brush insides with olive oil and place cut-side-up under the broiler in your oven. Broil 30 - 60 seconds until golden.
  5. Place buns on plate and spoon filling over the top. Add toppings if desired. Serve.
  6. Leftover filling keeps well in an airtight container in the refrigerator for 2-3 days.

Gluten-free option: Use gluten-free buns or you can go the bunless route - the filling is also great over brown rice or spaghetti squash.
