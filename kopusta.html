Kopusta Recipe

Prep Time: ~15 minutes | Cook Time: 1-4 hours

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 onion
  • 3 cloves of sliced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon caraway seeds
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 lb of kielbasa
  • 1 cup sauerkraut (reserve 1 oz of liquid)
  • 1 head of green cabbage
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 cups fresh chicken stock

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pot or ceramic Dutch oven.
  2. Add onion and cook until just browned.
  3. Add garlic and cook until aromatic.
  4. Add spices and cook until fragrant.
  5. Add sauerkraut and cook until a fond (Zuke) forms.
  6. Deglaze the pot with reserved sauerkraut liquid.
  7. Add kielbasa and cook for a few minutes.
  8. Add cabbage and then immediately add chicken stock.
  9. Fold all ingredients together in the pot.
  10. Simmer for an hour or place in the oven at 300Â°F for up to 4 hours.

Notes

  • Paprika is dried red bell pepper.
  • Bay leaves react with fat to remove greasy flavors.
  • Add a pinch of salt each time you add a new ingredient.
  • For authentic sauerkraut, visit D & D Polish Deli in Telford.
